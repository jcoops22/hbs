The Salon...

HairByShandel is a cozy salon in North Ogden. Here Shandel shares the space with her sister Crystal (NailArtByCrystal). Both are experienced professionals and take pride in offering quality work and making sure their clients are satisfied.

Shandel is beloved by her clients and always makes sure they love their look. Shandel prides herself on only using the best products for her clients because she knows how important it is. Shandel is currently only accepting waitlist appointments.
